The simplest definition of management is getting things done through people.

It implies that an organization, whether small, medium, or large, is composed of people.

A business organization exist for a purpose. All these business entities exist for a profit and have people.

It is the people in these organization that get the work done.

Management

Is a function that directs and coordinates the efforts of the people to accomplish goals and objectives by using available resources effectively and efficiently.

It is also a process of accomplishing the organization's goals by working with and through people.

Its task includes:

  • planning
  • organizing
  • staffing
  • directing and controlling
